Liv-ay Account Deletion

Last updated: July 19, 2026

If you wish to delete your Liv-ay account and the associated personal information and service data, you may request deletion using the procedure below. This page details the account deletion process referenced in Article 5 (Retention Period) and Article 7 (User Rights) of our Privacy Policy.

1. How to Request Deletion

Please request account deletion by email.

To: contact@cho-fam.com

Subject: [Liv-ay] Account Deletion Request

Please include the following in your message to help us verify your identity and process the request quickly:

  • The email address or phone number used at sign-up
  • Your in-service nickname (username)
  • If you signed up via social login (Google/Apple), the linked account information

2. Processing Procedure and Timeline

  1. Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity based on the information above.
  2. Once identity is verified, your account and personal information will be destroyed after a 24-hour grace period (the same standard set out in Article 5 of the Privacy Policy).
  3. You may withdraw your request during the grace period by contacting the same email address.
  4. Once destruction is complete, we will notify you at the email address you used to submit the request.

3. What Gets Deleted

Once account deletion is complete, the following information is destroyed in an unrecoverable manner.

  • Sign-up information: email address, phone number, linked social account information, nickname
  • Identity verification records: email/phone verification records, adult verification status
  • Service usage records: play records, created/registered content, currency transaction history, device identifier, access logs
  • Creator settlement information: registered payout account and related payment data

Any remaining coins, Gems, or cash balance are forfeited upon account deletion and cannot be recovered (Terms of Service, Article 6). Account deletion is irreversible, so please request carefully.
