PHILOSOPHY

Core Principles

Salaries are a means of living, not wealth accumulation. Everyone receives the same amount, and wealth is built through performance and contribution.

01

Equal Living Wage

All members receive the same base salary regardless of role. Pay is a tool for stability, not competition.

02

Independent Units

Each member operates as an independent business entity, managing their own revenue and expenses.

03

Contribution-Based

Shared costs are allocated proportionally based on each member's net contribution, with performance bonuses for exceeding targets.

04

Equity Accumulation

Surplus contributions are converted into family holding company shares, building long-term family assets.

STRUCTURE

How It Works

A transparent settlement and fair distribution system for your family enterprise.

Individual P&L

Each member's contribution is calculated transparently.

Net Contribution = Individual Revenue - Direct Costs - Shared Cost Allocation

Shared Cost Allocation

Common expenses such as office, marketing, and legal costs are allocated proportionally based on each member's contribution.

Shared costs are distributed proportionally to each member's net contribution

Bonuses & Equity

Contributions exceeding the baseline are split between immediate performance bonuses and long-term equity conversion.

Surplus → Partial bonus payout Surplus → Remaining as equity
